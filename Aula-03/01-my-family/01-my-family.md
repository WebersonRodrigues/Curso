# Texto – My Family

---

## 🇺🇸 Versão em Inglês

Hi! My name is Sophia. I am ten years old.

I live with my parents and my older brother. We call him Carlinhos.

I am short and thin. My brother is tall and very funny.

He is also the cutest one in the family — but don't tell him that!

I love learning new things and I am always ready for new adventures.

My mother's name is Patrícia. She takes care of our house.

She makes lunch, dinner, and breakfast for us.

She also washes and irons our clothes. They always smell amazing.

My father's name is Jonathan. He works very hard.

He is a carpenter and he is always taking care of us.

He leaves home at seven in the morning and comes back at seven at night.

He is very dedicated!

I love my family.

Yesterday, we went to Uncle Binho's house. He made pizza for everyone and it was delicious!

Today, we are staying home, watching a movie and eating popcorn.

Tomorrow, we are going to visit my grandmother's house. I can't wait!

### 🔑 Expressões e Sentenças Importantes

**"My mother's name is / My father's name is"** — minha mãe se chama / meu pai se chama
> My mother's name is Patrícia.
> *Forma natural em inglês para apresentar o nome de alguém: "name is", não "is called" (que também existe, mas é menos comum no A1).*

**"He is always taking care of us"** — ele está sempre cuidando da gente
> *Presente contínuo com "always" reforça um hábito constante ou característica da pessoa.*

**"They always smell amazing"** — elas ficam sempre cheirosíssimas
> *"Smell" como verbo de ligação: descreve uma qualidade sensorial, sem precisar de "be".*

**"Yesterday / Today / Tomorrow"** — ontem / hoje / amanhã
> Yesterday we went. / Today we are staying. / Tomorrow we are going.
> *Três tempos verbais diferentes em três frases seguidas — ótimo para praticar passado, presente e futuro.*

**"I can't wait!"** — mal posso esperar!
> *Expressão idiomática muito comum para demonstrar animação ou expectativa.*

**"Don't tell him that!"** — não conta para ele!
> *Imperativo negativo: "don't" + verbo base. Muito usado no inglês informal.*

### 💡 Como Aplicar no Dia a Dia

**1. Apresente sua família em inglês**
Use o modelo do texto para falar sobre as pessoas da sua casa:
> "My mother's name is ___. She ___."
> "My father's name is ___. He works as a ___."

**2. Descreva pessoas com adjetivos simples**
> "My brother is tall and funny."
> "My sister is short and very smart."

**3. Pratique os três tempos com yesterday / today / tomorrow**
Toda noite, tente resumir seu dia em inglês:
> "Yesterday I went to school. Today I am at home. Tomorrow I am going to my friend's house."

**4. Use "takes care of" para falar de responsabilidades**
> "My mother takes care of the house."
> "My teacher takes care of the students."

**5. Fale sobre o que você ama**
> "I love my family."
> "I love learning new things."
> "I love watching movies and eating popcorn."
