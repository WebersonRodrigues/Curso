# Texto 1 – My Birthday Party

## 📖 Texto – "My Birthday Party"

Today is my birthday! I am ten years old!

My mom **decorated** the living room with **balloons** and a big **banner** that says "Happy Birthday!"

There are blue, yellow, and red balloons everywhere. It looks so pretty!

My mom **baked** a chocolate cake. It has ten **candles** on top — one for each year. The cake has white **frosting** and colorful **sprinkles**.

At three o'clock, my **guests** start to arrive. I **invited** my friends from school: Pedro, Sofia, Lis, and Ana.

Each friend **brings** a **gift**. The gifts are **wrapped** in colorful paper with **ribbons**. I am so excited!

"Don't open them yet!" my mom **says**. "Wait until after the cake!"

We play games in the backyard. We play musical chairs and pass the parcel. Everyone **laughs** and has fun.

Then my mom **calls** us inside. It is time for the cake!

Everyone **sings**: "Happy birthday to you! Happy birthday to you! Happy birthday dear Sofia! Happy birthday to you!"

My mom **lights** the candles. The flames are small and bright.

"Make a **wish**!" everyone **says**.

I **close** my eyes and make a wish. I can't tell anyone — it's a secret!

Then I take a deep breath and **blow out** all ten candles at once! Everyone **claps** and **cheers**!

My mom **cuts** the cake and **serves** a piece to everyone. It is delicious!

After the cake, it is time to open my **gifts**!

I **unwrap** the first one. It is a book about animals! "Thank you, Pedro!"

The second gift is a set of colored pencils. "Thank you, Lis!"

Ana **gives** me a puzzle with 500 pieces. "Wow, thank you!"

And Sofia gives me a beautiful **bracelet**. "I love it! Thank you!"

I **hug** all my friends. This is the best birthday ever!

At the end of the party, my mom **gives** each guest a small **party bag** with candy and a toy inside.

"Thank you for coming!" I **say** to everyone.

"Happy birthday!" they all **say** as they leave.

I am tired but so happy. Ten years old! I can't wait for next year!

### 🧠 Estruturas gramaticais do texto

**1. "Make a wish" — faça um pedido**
> Make a wish! / I made a wish.

Expressão fixa usada em aniversários:
- Close your eyes and make a wish. (Feche os olhos e faça um pedido.)

---

**2. "Don't + verbo" — não + verbo (imperativo negativo)**
> Don't open them yet! / Don't tell anyone!

Forma de dar ordens negativas:
- Don't run! (Não corra!)
- Don't forget! (Não esqueça!)

---

**3. "I can't wait for…" — mal posso esperar por…**
> I can't wait for next year!

Expressa ansiedade positiva:
- I can't wait for the party! (Mal posso esperar pela festa!)
- I can't wait to open my gifts! (Mal posso esperar pra abrir meus presentes!)

---

### 🌍 Contexto cultural

- Nos EUA, a música de aniversário é **"Happy Birthday to You"** — a mesma melodia que no Brasil!
- É tradição **blow out the candles** (apagar as velas) e fazer um pedido secreto.
- **Party bags** (sacolinhas de festa) são muito comuns nos EUA — cada convidado leva uma pra casa com doces e brinquedinhos.
- Nos EUA, festas de aniversário de criança geralmente acontecem em casa, no parque, ou em lugares como bowling, trampoline parks, etc.
