📐 Estruturas Gramaticais Praticadas

  • ✅ Second Conditional — "If I could travel anywhere, I would go to Japan."
  • ✅ Past Continuous — "I was looking for my backpack when the bell rang."
  • ✅ Should / Shouldn't — "You should study every day." / "Kids shouldn't have too much homework."
  • ✅ Reported Speech (basic) — "She said she was nervous."
  • ✅ Giving opinions — "I think..." / "In my opinion..." / "I believe..."
  • ✅ Sequencing events — "First... Then... After that... Finally..."
  • ✅ Would + verb — "I would tell my younger self to be brave."

💡 Dicas para Revisar o Bloco 5

✅ Ouça os áudios várias vezes — quanto mais ouvir, mais natural fica

✅ Leia os textos em voz alta — ler em voz alta ajuda a internalizar as frases

✅ Faça shadowing dos textos — repita junto com o áudio, imitando o ritmo e a pronúncia

✅ Revise no Anki todo dia — a repetição espaçada fixa as frases na memória

✅ Escreva sua opinião sobre um tema em inglês — pratique usar "I think", "I believe", "In my opinion"
