📋 Guia da Aula 23

🎯Objetivo da Semana

Aprender os phrasal verbs mais usados no dia a dia (figure out, give up, turn off, pick up, hang out) e expressões para manter uma conversa fluindo (well, you know, I mean, actually, by the way).

Nível: B1→B2 — Você já entende contrações e agora vai dominar phrasal verbs e filler words — o segredo para soar natural em inglês!

📖Textos da Semana

1️⃣ Phrasal Verbs Are Everywhere

Tema: Phrasal verbs no dia a dia — como nativos usam verbos compostos em vez de palavras formais

Vocabulário: figure out, give up, look up, turn off, pick up, drop off, come up with, run out of, get along with, look forward to, put up with, show up, hang out, grow up

Duração: ~4-5 minutos de áudio

2️⃣ Keeping a Conversation Going

Tema: Filler words e expressões para manter conversas fluindo — well, you know, I mean, actually, by the way

Vocabulário: well, you know, I mean, actually, basically, by the way, anyway, so, right?, really?, that's interesting, what do you mean?, how about you?, tell me more

Duração: ~4-5 minutos de áudio

🧠Como Estudar os Textos

Passo 1: Compreensão Inicial

1. Leia o texto em português 3 vezes
2. Entenda a história/contexto completamente
3. Não se preocupe com inglês ainda

Passo 2: Listening com Texto

1. Escute o áudio junto com o texto em inglês (5 minutos)
2. Acompanhe cada palavra
3. Deixe o som entrar nos ouvidos

Passo 3: Listening com Tradução

1. Escute o áudio junto com o texto em português (5 minutos)
2. Conecte som com significado
3. Entenda o ritmo da fala

Passo 4: Listening Puro

1. Escute só o áudio em inglês (5 minutos)
2. Sem texto, sem tradução
3. Deixe o cérebro trabalhar

Passo 5: Anki (Memorização)

1. Adicione frase por frase no Anki
2. Cada frase com áudio
3. Revise diariamente

Passo 6: Shadowing (Fala)

1. Para cada frase do Anki, repita junto com o áudio
2. Tente 5 vezes por frase
3. Foco em pronúncia e ritmo

US English

Yesterday I was watching my favorite show in English, and a character said: "I can't figure out this puzzle!"

I thought: "Why didn't he say 'solve'?" So I asked my English teacher today.

She smiled and said: "Welcome to phrasal verbs! Native speakers use them all the time."

She explained that phrasal verbs are a verb plus a small word like up, out, on, or off — and together they create a new meaning.

I started paying attention, and now I hear them everywhere!

This morning, my mom said: "Turn off the TV and pick up your toys!"

My dad was leaving for work and said: "I need to drop off your sister at school."

At school, my teacher said: "Don't give up! You can do it!"

My friend Lucas couldn't answer a question, so he decided to look up the answer in the dictionary.

During lunch, my friend Sofia said: "I came up with a great idea for our project!"

I told her: "That's awesome! I look forward to hearing it!"

After school, I wanted to hang out with my friends at the park.

My mom said: "Sure, but we ran out of milk, so come home early."

At the park, my friend asked: "Do you get along with your little sister?"

I laughed and said: "Most of the time! But sometimes it's hard to put up with her singing all day!"

We talked about what we want to be when we grow up.

I said: "I want to be a game designer! I need to work out how to make my own games."

Then my friend Pedro finally showed up — he was late, as always!

I went home feeling happy. Now I understand — phrasal verbs are just how people really talk!

They're not difficult. They're like a treasure hunt — once you start looking, you find them everywhere!

🔤 Vocabulário Essencial

1. Figure out 🧩

Significado: Descobrir / resolver

Exemplo: "I can't figure out this problem"

Dica: Mais natural que "solve" na fala

2. Give up 🏳️

Significado: Desistir

Exemplo: "Don't give up! You can do it!"

Dica: Muito usado para motivar

3. Turn off 📴

Significado: Desligar (aparelho)

Exemplo: "Turn off the TV"

Dica: Oposto: turn on = ligar

4. Pick up 🤲

Significado: Pegar / buscar

Exemplo: "Pick up your toys"

Dica: Pegar do chão ou buscar alguém

5. Come up with 💡

Significado: Inventar / ter uma ideia

Exemplo: "She came up with a great plan"

Dica: Criar algo novo na mente

6. Run out of 🚫

Significado: Ficar sem

Exemplo: "We ran out of milk"

Dica: Acabou o estoque

7. Hang out 🏖️

Significado: Sair / passar tempo com

Exemplo: "I like to hang out with friends"

Dica: Informal — ficar junto

8. Show up 🚶

Significado: Aparecer / chegar

Exemplo: "He always shows up late"

Dica: Mais informal que "arrive"

9. Look forward to 🤩

Significado: Estar ansioso para

Exemplo: "I look forward to the weekend!"

Dica: Expectativa positiva

10. Grow up 🌱

Significado: Crescer

Exemplo: "What do you want to be when you grow up?"

Dica: Crescer de criança para adulto

US English

Last Saturday, I was at the park with my mom when a tourist came up to us.

He looked at his phone and said: "Well, excuse me... do you know how to get to the beach from here?"

I said: "Sure! Basically, you go straight and turn left at the big tree."

He smiled and said: "Oh, thank you! By the way, my name is Mark. I'm from Canada."

"Nice to meet you! I'm Binho. So, are you here on vacation?"

"Yes! Well, you know, I always wanted to visit Brazil. The food, the music... I mean, everything is amazing here!"

"Really? Tell me more! What's your favorite food so far?"

"Actually, I tried açaí yesterday and I loved it! How about you? Do you eat açaí every day?"

I laughed and said: "Not every day, but I love it too! Anyway, what else do you want to see here?"

"Well, I want to visit the old part of the city. You know, the historic buildings and churches."

"That's interesting! My school went there last month. It's really beautiful."

Mark said something I didn't understand, so I asked: "What do you mean?"

He said: "Oh, sorry! I mean, is it far from here?"

"Not really! It's about twenty minutes by bus."

"Great! By the way, your English is really good!"

"Thank you! Actually, I study with my uncle. He's my English teacher."

Mark said: "Well, I should get going. It was really nice talking to you!"

I looked at my mom and said: "Mom! I just had a real conversation in English!"

She smiled and said: "I'm so proud of you!"

I felt amazing. I didn't need to be perfect — I just needed to keep the conversation going!

🔤 Vocabulário Essencial

1. Well 🤔

Significado: Bom / Bem (para pensar)

Exemplo: "Well, I think it's a good idea"

Dica: Ganha tempo para pensar

2. You know 💬

Significado: Sabe (conectar ideias)

Exemplo: "You know, Brazil is beautiful"

Dica: Torna a fala mais natural

3. Actually 💡

Significado: Na verdade

Exemplo: "Actually, I'm from Brazil"

Dica: NÃO é "atualmente"!

4. By the way 🔄

Significado: A propósito

Exemplo: "By the way, my name is Mark"

Dica: Muda de assunto suavemente

5. I mean 🗣️

Significado: Quer dizer

Exemplo: "I mean, it's not far"

Dica: Corrige ou explica melhor

6. Really? 😮

Significado: Sério?

Exemplo: "Really? That's amazing!"

Dica: Mostra surpresa e interesse

7. How about you? 🔁

Significado: E você?

Exemplo: "I love pizza. How about you?"

Dica: Devolve a pergunta

8. What do you mean? ❓

Significado: O que você quer dizer?

Exemplo: "What do you mean? I didn't understand"

Dica: Pede esclarecimento

9. Anyway ➡️

Significado: Enfim / De qualquer forma

Exemplo: "Anyway, what were you saying?"

Dica: Volta ao assunto principal

10. That's interesting! 🌟

Significado: Que interessante!

Exemplo: "That's interesting! Tell me more"

Dica: Mostra que está ouvindo
