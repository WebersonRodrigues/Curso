# Texto 1 – My Classroom

## 📖 Texto – "My Classroom"

My classroom is on the second floor. It is big and has many windows.

There are 25 **desks** in the room. Each desk has a **chair**. My desk is near the window. I like it there because I can see the trees outside.

At the front of the room, there is a big **board**. My teacher **writes** on it with a **marker**. Sometimes she uses **chalk** on the old board.

Next to the board, there is a **clock** on the wall. I always **look** at it when I am bored!

On my desk, I have my things: a **pencil**, a **pen**, an **eraser**, a **ruler**, and my **notebook**. I also have a **pencil case** where I keep everything.

My **backpack** is on the floor, next to my chair. Inside, I have my **books**, my **lunchbox**, and a bottle of water.

My teacher's name is Miss Clara. She is very nice. She **explains** things slowly and always **helps** us when we don't understand.

In the morning, we have **math** and **science**. After recess, we have **English** and **art**. I like art the most because we can **draw** and **paint**.

At 10 o'clock, the **bell** rings. It is time for **recess**! We all run to the **playground**.

My best friend sits next to me. His name is Pedro. Sometimes we **share** our **pencils** and **erasers**.

At the end of the day, we **clean** our desks, **put** our things in our backpacks, and **wait** for the bell.

When the bell rings, we say: "Goodbye, Miss Clara!" and she says: "See you tomorrow!"

I like my classroom. It is where I learn new things every day.

| Inglês | Português | Dica |

| desk | carteira (escolar) | mesa do aluno |
| board | quadro | onde o professor escreve |
| marker | marcador / canetão | para quadro branco |
| chalk | giz | para quadro negro |
| pencil | lápis | "pencil case" = estojo |
| pen | caneta | para escrever com tinta |
| eraser | borracha | apaga o lápis |
| ruler | régua | para medir e fazer linhas |
| notebook | caderno | onde escreve as lições |
| pencil case | estojo | guarda lápis e canetas |
| backpack | mochila | leva os materiais |
| bell | sinal | toca no recreio e na saída |
| recess | recreio | hora de brincar! |
| playground | pátio | onde brinca no recreio |
| share | dividir / compartilhar | "share pencils" = dividir lápis |

---

### 🧠 Estruturas gramaticais do texto

**1. "There is / There are" — tem / há**
> There are 25 desks in the room. / There is a clock on the wall.

- There is (singular): There is a board. (Tem um quadro.)
- There are (plural): There are many windows. (Tem muitas janelas.)

---

**2. "It is time for…" — é hora de…**
> It is time for recess!

- It is time for lunch. (É hora do almoço.)
- It is time for bed. (É hora de dormir.)

---

**3. "I like… because…" — eu gosto de… porque…**
> I like art because we can draw and paint.

- I like recess because I can play. (Gosto do recreio porque posso brincar.)
- I like my desk because it is near the window. (Gosto da minha carteira porque fica perto da janela.)

---

### 🌍 Contexto cultural

- Nos EUA, alunos geralmente **não usam uniforme** em escolas públicas.
- O **recess** (recreio) dura cerca de 15-20 minutos e as crianças brincam no playground.
- Professoras são chamadas de **"Miss"** (solteira) ou **"Mrs."** (casada) + sobrenome.
- Nos EUA, as matérias são chamadas de **"subjects"**: math, science, English, art, PE (physical education).
